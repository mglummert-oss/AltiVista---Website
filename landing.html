About the AltiVista Project

AltiVista is a cutting-edge tourism initiative based in Portugal that brings the world closer to you. Our network of panoramic telescopes allows visitors to soak in breathtaking vistas, while our innovative virtual walks guide you through scenic routes with insights into landmarks and local history. Complement your adventures with our carefully curated selection of binoculars, ensuring you never miss a detail.
